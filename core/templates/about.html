Brief Bio

I am currently working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Sciences at COMSATS University, Islamabad (Abbottabad Campus), Pakistan. From Sep 2007 to Aug 2009, I was a Research Associate at COMSATS University, Abbottabad. Before that, I was Lecturer Computer Science at Government Postgraduate College No. 1 Abbottabad (2005 to 2007).

I received M.Sc(CS) degree in Computer Science from Hazara University, Mansehra, Pakistan in 2004 and then did MS-Computer Sciences from COMSATS University Abbottabad in 2009 respectively on Scholarship respectively. I got selected under the CAS-TWAS President Fellowship Program 2016 for Ph.D. Study funded by UNESCO at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) Hefei China. I completed my Ph.D. in Information and Communication Engineering with Distinction in Just 2.5 Years.
